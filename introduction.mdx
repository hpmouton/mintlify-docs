# Welcome to Our Development Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for our Laravel-based enterprise solutions. This documentation covers two primary products:

## E-Recruitment Platform

Our comprehensive e-recruitment solution streamlines the entire hiring process from job posting to onboarding. The platform facilitates efficient candidate tracking, automated screening, and collaborative hiring decisions.

**Key Features:**
- Customizable job posting and application workflows
- Applicant tracking system with pipeline visualization
- Integrated assessment tools and interview scheduling
- Analytics and reporting for hiring metrics

## Learning Management System with Skill Audit

Our LMS platform goes beyond traditional learning management by integrating skill assessment and competency tracking. This allows organizations to align learning pathways with skills gaps and business objectives.

**Key Features:**
- Course creation and management
- Skill matrix mapping and competency frameworks
- Personalized learning paths based on skill gaps
- Progress tracking and certification management
